ETHNIC DAY

The most vibrant celebration of culture and style!

Date :28th October 2024
Time :10 AM Onwards
Venue :E-Block Gallery

Ethnic Day is a vibrant celebration of cultural diversity and heritage. It is a day to honor and showcase the richness of traditions, attire, music, dance, and customs from various ethnic backgrounds. The event brings together people from different walks of life to embrace and appreciate cultural differences, fostering unity and inclusivity. The day is a reminder of the importance of preserving our roots and passing on traditions to future generations. By celebrating each culture's uniqueness, Ethnic Day emphasizes unity in diversity, building a sense of togetherness and harmony.

Wear your ethnic attire and join us for a day full of fun, music, dance, and cultural celebrations.
